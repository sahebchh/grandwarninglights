Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1981

GRAND by IJS Electronics (ISO 9001:2015) has been a trusted pioneer in Emergency Vehicle Warning Products and Public Address Systems since 1981. Founded by S. Indarjit Singh Chhabra and expanded under Jaspal Singh Chhabra, we introduced India's first integrated Emergency Light Bar + PA/Siren System in 1988. With years of excellence and 29 design patents, GRAND is the go-to brand for police, fire, ambulance, and disaster response solutions.

1981
Founded in New Delhi
S. Indarjit Singh Chhabra establishes IJS Electronics in Delhi — bringing 35 years of expertise from Ahuja's Audio Centre, Chandni Chowk, to build a world-class Public Address System manufacturer.
1988
First Light Bar in India
Jaspal Singh introduces the Emergency Light Bar cum PA/Siren System concept — a first in India — under the GRAND brand.
2000
LED Technology Pioneer
Introduced LED Flashing Lights and LED Light Bars for the first time in India.
2010
GRAND Designs & ER One
Launched exclusive emergency vehicle design studio and the ER One Emergency Response Vehicle. Introduced Alertronic Differential Sound Siren.
2025
29 Patents & Counting
IJS Electronics now holds 29 design patents, operates 3 manufacturing plants, and supplies India's most critical emergency fleets.
ISO 9001:2015 Quality Certified
International quality management certification ensuring zero-defect manufacturing and nil failure rates under warranty. Products carry 1 to 3 year warranties depending on model.
29 Registered Design Patents
Over decades of R&D, GRAND holds 29 Government of India design patents for unique product designs including LED Light Bars, LED Disc, LED Disc Tower, Rotating Lights, and Speakers.
Ambulance AIS 125 Compliant
Products fully compliant with India's AIS 125 automotive standards for ambulance warning systems. Approved by Maruti, Tata Motors, Force Motors, and Ashok Leyland for factory-fit ambulances.
Delhi & Noida Facilities
State-of-the-art 20,000 sq. ft. facilities in Delhi & Noida supply major government tenders, OEM partners including Tata Motors, Force Motors, and Maruti Suzuki, and emergency service departments across India.
Presidential to State Level Deployment
GRAND products are trusted by President of India pilot vehicles, Prime Minister escort fleets, all major state Police, Fire, and Ambulance departments across India.
